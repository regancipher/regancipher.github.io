## Testing Process

### 1. Initial Impressions (Days 1-3)
Fresh ears, stock configuration, first reactions to build quality, comfort, and out-of-the-box sound.

### 2. Measurement Phase (Day 3-5)
Multiple measurements for consistency, comparison against target curves and competitors.

### 3. Extended Listening (Week 1-2)
Daily use across different genres, volumes, and sources. Testing durability, comfort over time, and versatility.

### 4. Final Analysis
Synthesizing measurements and impressions into recommendations for specific use cases and listener preferences.
